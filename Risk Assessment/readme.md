# Oil & Gas Cyber Risk Assessment Methodology

## Purpose
Provide a repeatable method for assessing IT, OT, cloud, AI, vendor, and operational cybersecurity risks across oil and gas environments.

## Risk Formula
Risk Score = Likelihood x Impact

Likelihood and impact are scored from 1 to 5. Risks scoring 15-25 are high priority.

## Impact Categories
- Safety impact
- Environmental impact
- Production downtime
- Regulatory exposure
- Financial loss
- Data confidentiality
- Integrity of operational decisions
- Reputation

## Example Risk Areas
- SCADA remote access exposure
- Ransomware impacting terminal scheduling
- Compromised vendor technician account
- Cloud data lake misconfiguration
- Unauthorized AI use with sensitive production data
- Incomplete backup restoration testing

## Risk Treatment Options
Accept, mitigate, transfer, or avoid. High risks require documented remediation owners and dates.
